Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 tablespoons of butter

Instructions

  1. Cook the bacon in a skillet until crispy.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place the bread, butter-side-down, in the skillet.
  4. Add a slice of cheddar cheese and a few tomato slices on top of the bread.
  5. Top with another slice of bread, butter-side-up.
  6. Cook the sandwich on each side until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  7. Remove from the skillet and let cool for a few minutes before cutting in half and serving.
